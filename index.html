About This Series

This lecture blog series is designed for students in tertiary institutions studying Organisational Communication as part of their academic programme. Each topic is presented as a self-contained blog lecture that can be read independently or followed sequentially as a complete course.

Every lecture includes detailed concept explanations with key concept boxes, real-world workplace examples drawn from Nigerian organisational settings, references to contemporary academic sources (2020 onwards), structured time allocations for reading and study, and examination practice questions with mark allocations to help students prepare for assessments.

How to Use This Series

Each lecture is self-contained and can be read independently. However, the topics are sequenced to build progressively — from foundational concepts through specific communication types and challenges, to practical integrative strategies.

📖
Read

Follow the time allocation provided — read actively, not passively. Engage with the key concept boxes and workplace examples.

📝
Note

Take notes on key definitions, concepts, and examples. Identify connections between topics as the series progresses.

✏️
Practise

Attempt the examination questions at the end of each lecture. Use the mark allocations to guide the depth and length of your answers.
